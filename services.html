What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive clinical research consulting across all phases of drug and device development.

🧬

Clinical Trial Protocol Design

We develop rigorous, regulatory-ready clinical study protocols for Phase I through IV trials, tailored to your molecule, therapeutic area, and target market.

  • Study design and endpoint selection
  • Statistical power calculations and sample sizing
  • Inclusion/exclusion criteria development
  • Patient population and site selection strategy
  • Protocol review and scientific critique
🔬

Clinical Monitoring & Oversight

We provide expert clinical monitoring services to ensure your investigational sites operate in full compliance with GCP guidelines.

  • Remote and on-site monitoring visits (SDV, SDR, RMM)
  • Site initiation, interim, and close-out visits
  • Protocol deviation tracking and management
  • Safety reporting and pharmacovigilance support
  • Investigator and site staff training
📋

Regulatory Affairs Consulting

Strategic regulatory guidance to accelerate your path to market across FDA, EMA, and international health authorities.

  • IND, NDA, BLA, and MAA submission strategy
  • Orphan drug designation applications
  • Fast Track, Breakthrough Therapy, PRIME designations
  • Regulatory agency meeting preparation
  • CTD / eCTD dossier review and gap analysis
📝

Medical Writing

High-quality scientific and regulatory documents prepared by experienced medical writers.

  • Clinical Study Reports (CSR) per ICH E3
  • Investigator Brochures (IB)
  • Patient Information Leaflets and Informed Consent Forms
  • Scientific manuscripts for peer-reviewed publication
  • Summary documents and regulatory overviews
📊

Biostatistics & Data Management

Statistical expertise to support trial planning, execution, and analysis.

  • Statistical Analysis Plan (SAP) review and development
  • Sample size and power calculations
  • Randomization scheme design
  • Data management plan oversight (EDC, Medidata, CRIO)
  • DSMB/DMC statistical support
