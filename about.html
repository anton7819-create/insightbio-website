Who We Are

Built on a Decade of Pharmaceutical Expertise

INSIGHTBIO, S.A. was founded in 2026 in Panama City, Republic of Panama, by a team of clinical research professionals with more than 10 years of combined experience in the pharmaceutical and biotechnology sector.

The company was established to formalize and scale an international consulting practice built over years of hands-on clinical trial work across the UAE, Hong Kong, the United States, and Europe.

Operating on a fully remote and on-site B2B model, INSIGHTBIO delivers high-quality consulting services to pharmaceutical sponsors and CROs worldwide.
